There’s and old Chinese saying: “Reflect on your past to discover your future.” Now look back on your career to date by constructing a timeline. Discover strengths and weaknesses, pinnacles and troughs, and defining moments in your career.

My Journey @ Work

Four key questions

Think about the highs and lows of your work life in the past week or month. Look at each major incident and ask:

  1. What happened?
  2. Why did it happen?
  3. Where was Jesus when it happened? What was he doing?
  4. What did you learn from that incident?
  • Write down key words and ideas that come to mind.
  • Speak quietly with Jesus as you walk through each even again.
  • Be honest. This is strictly confidential.
  • Based on what you have discovered during your reflection, what have you learned about yourself?
  • How will you move on?
